About HiddenPath Explorer

Your gateway to the unexplored Northeast India

HiddenPath Explorer is dedicated to bringing the hidden treasures of Northeast India to travelers worldwide. Our platform provides real-time insights, crowd predictions, and curated experiences to help you discover the region's natural beauty, cultural richness, and adventure opportunities.

From the living root bridges of Meghalaya to the pristine valleys of Arunachal Pradesh, we connect you with authentic local experiences while promoting sustainable tourism and cultural preservation.

Our Mission

To make Northeast India accessible and enjoyable for every traveler, fostering economic growth and cultural exchange.

Our Vision

A world where technology bridges the gap between urban explorers and remote destinations.
